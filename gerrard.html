Steven Gerrard is an English footballer who plays for Liverpool FC. He is the current captain for both club and country. Steven is known as one of the best midfielders of his generation and has won many trophies with his club. Perhaps his greatest honour was captaining his club side to triumph in the 2005 UEFA Champions League, where Liverpool came from 3-0 down at half time to beat AC Milan in a penalty shoot-out in the final. He has also won the FA Cup, League Cup and UEFA Cup with his club.
