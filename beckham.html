David Beckham is an English footballer who has played for Manchester United, Real Madrid, AC Milan and Los Angeles Galaxy. He is a former England captain. He holds the appearance record for an outfield player for the England National Team. He has also won many trophies in his long career. With Manchester United, he won the Premier League title six times, the FA Cup twice, and the UEFA Champions League in 1999. He left to join Real Madrid in 2003, where he remained for four seasons, winning the La Liga championship in his final season with the club.
