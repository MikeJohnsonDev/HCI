Jessica Ennis is a British olympic heptathlete. She competed and won gold for Team GB at the London 2012 Olympic games with her career best ever points total. She is also the former European and world heptathlon champion and the former world indoor pentathlon champion. She is the current British national record holder for the heptathlon, the indoor pentathlon, the high jump and the 100 metres hurdles.
