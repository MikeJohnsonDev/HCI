Zinedine Zidane is a French former footballer who has played for many clubs including Real Madrid, Juventus and the French national team. He is considered one of the games greatest ever players and has won the UEFA Champions League with Real Madrid, where he scored the winning goal, and league titles with Juventus. He is famous for head butting Marco Materazzi of Italy in the 2006 World Cup final, for which he received a red card.
