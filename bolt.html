Usain Bolt is an olympic sprinter specializing in the 100m and 200m. He is a 6-time olympic gold medallist and the current world record holder widely considered the greatest sprinter of all time. He recently won gold in the 100m, 200m and 4x100m relay sprints at the London 2012 olympic games, successfully defending the titles he won in Beijing in 2008.
