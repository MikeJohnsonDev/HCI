The Museum is delighted to announce the appointment of Justin Morris as Director of Public Engagement.

Justin is currently Director of Operations and Projects at the British Museum, where he has worked for most of the past 17 years, with a two year period working for the John Lewis Partnership fast-track senior management training scheme. 'I am delighted to be joining the Natural History Museum to lead the continuing development of its public engagement programme, says Justin.
