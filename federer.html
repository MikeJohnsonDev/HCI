Roger Federer is a professional tennis player from Switzerland. Many sports analysts, tennis critics and former and current players consider Federer the greatest tennis player of all time. He has won 17 grand slam tennis titles, which is the all-time record. He is a 7-time winner of Wimbledon, 5 of those coming in consecutive years, which is a joint all-time record.
